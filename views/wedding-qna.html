How do we get to the venue?

Download the Grab app and pin "Vows Chapelle" as your destination in the app.

Where should we fly into?

The closest airport is Don Mueang International Airport (DMK), about an hour drive from the venue. You may also fly into Bangkok Suvarnabhumi International Airport (BKK) which has better connections from international locations.

What time should we arrive?

Please plan to arrive at the ceremony site no later than 5pm. It will take some time to get from the center of Bangkok to the venue during rush hour, so please plan ahead! For those driving to the venue, there will be available parking space.
Important: Plan extra time for rush hour traffic when traveling from central Bangkok to the venue.

What is the dress code?

Our wedding will be formal cocktail attire. Thailand is very hot and humid throughout the year, but January is considered Thai winter, which is cooler and drier.
Weather: Temperatures range from 25°C to 30°C during the day and 16°C to 24°C at night.
