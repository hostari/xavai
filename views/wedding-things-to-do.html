CHINATOWN BANGKOK

Eat like a local, regret like a tourist. Hit up Yaowarat (Chinatown) for dishes so spicy they'll help you forget your wedding speech faux pas. Try something unidentifiable on a stick. Don't ask, just eat.

WAT ARUN RATCHAWARARAM RATCHAWARAMAHAWIHAN

The temple that makes you go "whoa" in person and "how do I edit the sky to look like that?" on Instagram. A.k.a. Bangkok's majestic morning selfie spot, historical wonder, and cardio workout disguised as sightseeing.

ICONSIAM

Bangkok's glitziest shrine to shopping, dining, culture, and "Wow, do I need a $4,000 handbag?" vibes. So huge with river view you'll lose track of time, your friends, and probably your will to live. You can also go on a lunch or dinner buffet on a River Cruise here which you can book a week in advance.

RAJADAMNERN MUAY THAI STADIUM

Watch as two people kick each other's souls out, while you sip a beer and cheer like you know what's happening.

KHAO SAN ROAD

If you thought the wedding reception got rowdy, Khao San will laugh in your face. Buckets of alcohol, neon body paint, and the ever-haunting memory of someone offering you a deep-fried scorpion.

SOI COWBOY

Bangkok's neon-lit nightlife strip where the music's loud, the drinks are strong, and the stories you'll tell are highly questionable. Three blocks of vibrant chaos, flashing lights, and the kind of people-watching that makes you question everything. What happens on Soi Cowboy stays on Soi Cowboy (but probably ends up on someone's Instagram anyway).

TRAIN NIGHT MARKET (TALAD ROTFAI)

Where vintage vibes meet street food paradise. Rummage through retro treasures you don't need, devour grilled meats on skewers, and pretend you're cooler than you actually are while surrounded by antique cars and old-school memorabilia. It's hipster heaven with a side of pad thai.

JURASSIC WORLD - THE EXPERIENCE

Get as close to living dinosaurs as you'll ever want to be (without the whole "getting eaten" part). This immersive 45-60 minute adventure at Asiatique lets you walk through cinematic scenes, dodge Stygimoloch encounters, and pretend you're way braver than you actually are. Pro tip: Save room for dinosaur-themed dining at Fossil & Flame after you've survived.
